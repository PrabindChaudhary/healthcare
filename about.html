About MediCare AI

A SaaS-based intelligent healthcare system — Chapter 1 Introduction to the Future of Medicine

Introduction

Revolutionizing Healthcare with AI & SaaS

Healthcare has always been an important part of human life. Without good health, a person's quality of life is greatly affected. With the increasing population and limited healthcare resources, there is a strong need for a proper and efficient healthcare system that can provide timely medical assistance.

MediCare AI is a Software as a Service (SaaS) based online healthcare system designed to allow patients to enter their personal details and symptoms through an online form. An AI-powered chatbot performs an initial online diagnosis based on the symptoms provided.

Depending on the diagnosis and the critical condition of the patient, the system automatically prioritizes patients according to urgency and disease severity — reducing mortality rates caused by delayed treatment.

Technology Stack

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript PHP 8 MySQL C (Urgency Engine) AI Chatbot API SaaS Platform

Problem Statement

Many existing healthcare systems lack an efficient mechanism for early symptom evaluation and patient prioritization. This delay can result in worsening health conditions and increased mortality rates.

Goals

Project Objectives

The main goals driving the development of MediCare AI

Online Healthcare System

Develop a comprehensive online system that collects patient details and symptoms digitally.

AI-Based Chatbot Diagnosis

Implement an AI chatbot using an external API for preliminary online diagnosis of symptoms.

Smart Patient Prioritization

Prioritize patients based on the severity and urgency of their medical condition automatically.

Online Appointment Booking

Enable seamless online appointment booking with verified doctors across specialties.

Paid Doctor Consultations

Provide a paid feature for online doctor consultations with prescriptions and medical advice.

Reduce Treatment Delays

Reduce delays in diagnosis and treatment through digital healthcare services and early intervention.

Approach

Development Methodology

01

Requirement Analysis

Identify user requirements, system features, and healthcare workflow needs.

02

System Design

Design user interfaces for patient registration, symptom entry, chatbot, and appointment booking.

03

Backend Development

Develop PHP server-side logic for data storage, symptom analysis, and patient management.

04

AI Chatbot Integration

Integrate an AI chatbot using external API to analyze symptoms and provide preliminary diagnoses.

05

Doctor Module

Create a doctor module for viewing appointments, consulting patients online, and prescriptions.

06

Testing & Deployment

Perform functional and usability testing, then deploy the system as a SaaS platform.

Results
